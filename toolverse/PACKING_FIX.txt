PACKING INDEX.HTML — REQUIRED CHANGES
======================================

In the file: packing/public/packing/index.html

Find and replace these 3 lines near the bottom of the file:

BEFORE:
  <script src="/toolverse/packing/js/api.js"></script>
  <script src="/toolverse/packing/js/packer3d.js"></script>
  <script src="/toolverse/packing/js/app.js"></script>

AFTER:
  <script src="/packing/js/api.js"></script>
  <script src="/packing/js/packer3d.js"></script>
  <script src="/packing/js/app.js"></script>

That's it — just remove "/toolverse" prefix from the 3 script paths.

Also check packing/js/api.js — if it calls "/toolverse/api/pack" or similar,
change it to "/api/pack".
